Services

Services

Some of the services offered in English at Shree P. M. Patel High School include:

  1. 1. English Language Classes: We provide English language classes for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. These classes focus on improving students' speaking, listening, reading, and writing skills in English.
  1. 2. English Literature Courses: We offer a variety of English literature courses that explore classic and contemporary works of literature. These courses aim to enhance students' understanding and appreciation of English literature.
  1. 3. Writing Workshops: Our writing workshops help students develop their writing skills in English. These workshops cover various types of writing, such as essays, reports, and creative writing, and provide students with feedback and guidance to improve their writing abilities.
  1. 4. English Conversation Club: The English conversation club provides a platform for students to practice speaking in English in a relaxed and informal setting. Students can engage in conversations on different topics and improve their fluency and communication skills.
  1. 5. English Tutoring: For students who need extra assistance in English, we offer one-on-one tutoring sessions. These sessions are tailored to suit each student's individual needs and focus on areas where they require additional support.
  1. 6. English Language Exams Preparation: We provide guidance and preparation for English language exams, such as IELTS, TOEFL, and Cambridge English exams. Our experienced teachers help students develop the necessary skills and strategies to excel in these exams.
  1. 7. English Cultural Activities: To expose students to English-speaking cultures, we organize various cultural activities. These activities include drama performances, debates, and presentations, allowing students to practice their language skills while learning about different cultures.
  1. 8.Overall, our English services aim to enhance students' language proficiency, communication skills, and overall understanding of the English language and literature.

About SHREE P. M. PATEL HIGH SCHOOL

  • 1956

    Our Humble Beginnings

    1. The SHREE P. M. PATEL HIGH SCHOOL, which was established in 1956, is a co-educational institution located in the rural area of GODHRA block in PANCH MAHALS district, Gujrat. Managed by Pvt. Aided, this school offers grades 9 to 12 but does not have a pre-primary section. The school is of non-Ashram type (Govt.) and follows Gujarati as the medium of instruction. Situated on an all-weather road, the school can be easily accessed by students and teachers. The academic session starts in April, marking the beginning of a new educational year.

  • Private

    Building

    1. The school has a private building that houses 10 classrooms, all of which are in good condition and used for instructional purposes. In addition to the classrooms, there are 2 other rooms dedicated to non-teaching activities, and a separate room for the Headmaster/Teacher. The school is surrounded by a pucca boundary wall, providing security and a sense of ownership to the institution. It also benefits from an electric connection, ensuring uninterrupted power supply.

  • Drinking

    Water

    1. For basic amenities, the school has a reliable source of tap water, ensuring students have access to clean and safe drinking water. Moreover, there are separate functional toilets for both boys and girls, with 7 toilets designated for boys and 5 toilets for girls.

  • Activities

    Physical and Leisure Activities

    1. To cater to the students' physical and leisure activities, the school boasts a playground where students can engage in various sports and recreational activities. Additionally, there is a well-stocked library with 1441 books, promoting a reading culture among the students and providing them with resources for academic and personal development. Regarding technological infrastructure, the school possesses 11 computers, all of which are functional and utilized for teaching and learning purposes. However, it does not have a computer-aided learning lab, which can further enhance students' exposure to digital and technological resources.

  • Drinking

    Water

    1. Although the school does not provide mid-day meals, it offers a conducive and well-equipped environment for students to pursue their education and holistic growth. It is an institution dedicated to providing quality education to its students in the rural area, nurturing their intellectual development and preparing them for a bright future.
